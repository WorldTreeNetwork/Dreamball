# 2026-04-21 — Vril flow model: plenum abundance, monotone strength, rendered freshness

## Status

Accepted. Closes PRD `docs/products/memory-palace/prd.md §9` open
question *"Vril conservation"*.

## Context

The Memory Palace carries **Vril** on every `jelly.aqueduct` (PRD §5.4)
— the life-force substance that makes the palace feel alive instead
of inert. The PRD §9 open question asked whether Vril is:

- **endogenous** — conserved within the palace, generated by the
  fountain, consumed by stasis; or
- **exogenous** — supplied by the wayfarer's attention; a palace
  unvisited for weeks drains to dark.

The authoritative clarification (2026-04-21, user) resolves the
question with a **plenum** model:

> "Vril itself, coming in and out of being, does seem like it is
> related to consciousness, to attention, and interaction, as well as
> the magic that's locked into a space or a particular DreamBall.
> Vril can flow. It's never destroyed, but it can go back into the
> space of pure potentiality — the zero-point field — so it can leave
> a space just as it enters. We are in a plenum: this jelly stuff can
> be shaped. There's no scarcity, it is abundant. It's not infinite.
> It still rises and falls and is used and replenished."

Plus the direction on visual decay:

> "Dimmed by time. Cobwebs, dust. You can see it needs a little
> sprucing up."

The design question therefore splits cleanly into two parts: **what is
conserved on the wire** (the signed record), and **what is rendered**
(the lived-in feeling).

## Decision

### Substrate model

1. **Vril is never destroyed.** Flow is symmetric: Vril enters a
   space and can return to the zero-point field of pure potentiality.
   Entering and leaving are the same mechanism in opposite phase.

2. **Plenum, not infinite.** Vril is abundant (no scarcity), but it
   rises and falls and is used and replenished. The zero-point field
   is the reservoir; the palace is a shape that sources draw into and
   release back to.

3. **Inflow is twofold.** A palace receives Vril from:
   - **Endogenous — fountain baseline.** A small constant inflow at
     the palace's zero point, regardless of occupancy. This honours
     "the magic that's locked into a space."
   - **Exogenous — attention-proportional.** Wayfarer traversals,
     inscriptions, oracle dialogue, and observer gaze each contribute
     proportional inflow. This honours "related to consciousness,
     attention, and interaction."

### Wire-format contract (signed record)

4. **Aqueduct `strength` is monotone on the signed chain.** Strength
   only increases as traversals accumulate; the signed timeline is a
   faithful record of every fire-together-wire-together event. A
   palace is never made to *lie about its past* by having its signed
   strength decremented. This matches "Vril is never destroyed" at
   the wire layer.

5. **`last-traversed` stays as specified (PRD §5.4).** Monotone clock
   attribute, updated on every traversal. This is the single hinge
   the renderer uses to compute freshness.

6. **No new wire primitives.** Plenum inflow, fountain baseline, and
   zero-point release are all *computed* by the runtime from the
   signed action history (per PRD §5.4's note that Vril is measured,
   not declared). Signed causes; measured effects.

### Rendering contract (lived-in feeling)

7. **Freshness is a derived, renderer-side attribute.** Computed per
   aqueduct as a function of `(now - last-traversed)`, with a
   configurable half-life. Default half-life: **30 days** to the
   "dusty" visual threshold, **90 days** to "cobwebs", **365 days**
   to "sleeping" (ghost-luminance only). These numbers are
   renderer-side tunables, not wire-format values.

8. **Freshness modulates the render, not the data.** A palace
   returned to after six months shows the full topology it earned —
   dimmed, dusty, cobwebbed — and the first fresh traversal
   instantly relights the aqueduct it touches. The signed history is
   never mutated by the visit of time; only the *rendered glow* is.

9. **Return-to-zero-point is visual, not destructive.** When an
   aqueduct's freshness drops to its floor, the renderer draws its
   Vril as returning to the dream-field ambient — particles drifting
   back toward an ambient sink rather than flowing along the path.
   This is the visual expression of "Vril can leave a space just as
   it enters."

### Oracle behaviour

10. **Oracle treats freshness as a reasoning signal.** The oracle
    MAY suggest revisiting dim rooms, surface forgotten inscriptions,
    or propose Vril-bottleneck shortcuts (PRD FR98, Vision tier). The
    oracle never asks the palace to lie — its suggestions are about
    *renewing* fresh Vril inflow, not about rewriting strength.

### Deferred to Vision tier

11. **Formal numerical Vril conservation** (balance-sheet accounting
    of inflow / pooling / outflow per room, whole-palace Vril
    throughput as a measurable quantity) is explicitly out of scope
    for MVP. The metaphor stays *proportional*; the unit system stays
    0–1 floats on aqueducts. Formal conservation and Vril bottleneck
    diagnostics (PRD FR98) are Vision tier.

## Consequences

- **No new envelope types.** The nine auxiliary envelopes in PRD §5.10
  are sufficient. `jelly.aqueduct`'s existing `strength`,
  `capacitance`, `conductance`, and `last-traversed` fields carry the
  whole model; freshness is derived.

- **Renderer pack gains a freshness shader.** NFR14's shader budget
  (≤4 new materials) now has a clear assignment: `aqueduct-flow`
  takes a `freshness` uniform; `room-pulse` takes a `freshness`
  uniform; `ley-line-ghost` is unchanged; a new `dust-cobweb`
  material overlays at the dim end. Within budget.

- **Oracle reasoning.** The oracle MAY use freshness as one input
  into its reflection ritual (PRD FR60e) — rooms whose freshness has
  dropped below a threshold since the last true-naming are candidates
  the oracle surfaces. Non-normative; falls into the existing
  Growth-tier `reflect` behaviour.

- **No CRDT merge hazards.** Because strength is monotone and
  freshness is a pure function of `last-traversed`, two wayfarers
  reconciling a shared room's aqueducts cannot disagree about Vril
  state — they can only disagree about which traversals happened and
  when, which is already the `jelly.action` DAG problem covered by
  PRD FR68.

- **Rebuild-from-CAS.** Freshness is recomputable from signed
  actions; a palace rebuilt from CAS (PRD FR84, Vision) sees the same
  rendered liveliness as the original, deterministically.

- **Phase 1 impact.** No wire-format change needed. The freshness
  derivation lands in the Svelte lens pack alongside the shader pack.
  The ingestion path (`src/memory-palace/store.ts`) keeps
  `last-traversed` current on every aqueduct row; nothing else
  changes.
